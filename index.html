About US

Pioneering bakery technology since 1998

Our Story

Founded in 1998 by master baker and engineer Robert Evans, BakeTech began with a simple mission: to create bakery machinery that combines precision engineering with practical baking expertise.

What started in a small workshop has grown into an internationally recognized brand trusted by artisan bakeries and industrial operations alike. Our commitment to innovation has resulted in 12 patented technologies that have transformed commercial baking operations worldwide.

Today, with facilities in three countries and a network of technical support specialists, we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in bakery automation while maintaining our core values of quality, reliability, and exceptional customer service.

Precision Engineering

Continuous Innovation

Exceptional Support
